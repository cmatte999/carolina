Copier Tool

For the Missionary Training Center

Built copier tool in Python to automate the process of copying a template course into a permanent course page in Canvas for the Missionary Training Center. Called the Canvas API to make automations in the language courses, reducing our copying process time by half.

BCC Bot

For the Business Career Center at BYU

Created automation in Python to send out survey links, reminders, and office events. The bot integrates with messaging platforms such as Microsoft Teams to send out messages to BCC employees and managers at scheduled times. Creates a database to store employee details, survey responses, and event RSVPs. Currently working on setting up user-friendly interface for employees to interact with the bot and update their information or preferences.

Data on Recruiter Satisfaction

For the Business Career Center at BYU

Created automation that imports data from two Qualtrics reports on recruiter satisfaction surveys, manipulates the data to extract relevant information, and saves the resulting dataframe to a Google Sheet. The code uses several libraries to achieve this goal, including pandas for data manipulation, gspread to interface with Google Sheets, and dropbox to download the survey reports. Specifically, the code downloads the two survey reports from Dropbox, converts them into pandas dataframes, cleans the data, and extracts relevant columns of data. It then saves the resulting dataframe to a Google Sheet.

Droplets

For the Business Career Center at BYU

Created automation that sends emails containing Droplets (customized messages from BCC managers and colleagues) to designated recipients. The script connects to a MySQL database, reads Droplet information from a table, creates an email for each recipient, and sends the email to the corresponding email address.

PantryPal

Personal Project

Currently working on building an app called PantryPal. The app uses OCR technology (Optical Character Recognition) to scan the text on user's grocery receipts and identify the product names, prices, and other details. The app will then use these details to access popular grocery stores' online catalogs and match the serial number of the products in the receipt scanned. With machine learning, it will use that information to filter only food items from the receipt, which are then logged in the app's database for nutrional information tracking and recipe recommendations based on logged ingredients. Currently exploring options to get funding opportunities with the BYU Ballard Center.
